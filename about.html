who are we

Ganpati Earth Movers is found in 2008 and has been growing steadily, since then despite of hardships faced by new players in infrastructure Development Industry. With years of experience working with construction companies, engineers and governments, GEM has developed broad expetise in the road infrastructure industry. Our technical experts can show you how to reduce cost, increase performance and road longevity, and improve roadway saftey.

Ganpati Earth Movers is active in the construction and maintainance of road and transport infrastructure and urban facilities. Construction of rigid and flexible pavement for road/highways and airfields, including bridges, flyovers, embankment with reinforced earth and earthwork.

We have a very good team of employees and a perfect work culture to supplement our journey to success. We are open for business with reputed clients for a long lasting and mutually rewarding relation.
